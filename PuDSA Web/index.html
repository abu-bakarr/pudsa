Pujehun District Students' Association

The Pujehun District Students’ Association (PuDSA) was formed to promote solidarity among Students from Pujehun district and to mobilize stakeholders, citizens, and students to foster development in the district. Since its inception, PuDSA’s objectives have been embraced with concrete support from key stakeholders in the district. PuDSA organizes its traditional events which includes the commemoration of the day of African Child, Annual General Meeting (AGM), Outing, Dinner and Awards, etc. including fund raising activities to implement projects like campaign against teenage pregnancy, debates and award of scholarships to outstanding primary and secondary school pupils and less privileged students.

As a student union association, PuDSA has always deemed it prudent to inject a new sense of purpose into the minds of its citizens to increase sustainable access to quality education in Pujehun district.The idea behind the formation of the association around 1970s was as a result of the outcry of students healing from Pujehun District regarding the promotion of their rights to educational opportunity and other social cohesion. The memberships of this association are students who are pursuing courses in Universities, Colleges and other Tertiary Institutions of learning in and outside Sierra Leone.Our value is motivated by our love for our district and a common desire to foster unity among students, promote academic excellence, cater for the welfare of members and collaborate with other stakeholders to promote developmentin Pujehun District. The Association is however, apolitical, nonsectarian, social and humanitarian Association which is governs by a Constitution as an Association. The association also stood firmed to cascade the awareness raising and sensitization campaigns on the need for peace and unity among citizens during the rebel war and the adherence of health care emergency to early combat of the Ebola outbreaks in Sierra Leone in order for Pujehun District to be united to enhance peace and stability among stakeholders, students and the entire citizenship of Pujehun District. In the recent 2018 elections, the association also trained and conducted voter’s education and nonviolence electioncampaigns in the district. The outcomes of these interventions vividly embarked on the needs for sustainable transformation of the district to a more productive and sustainable utilization of educational, economic, social and effective service delivery among duty bearers and also foster welfare diversification that will enhance positive change to the prevailing circumstances; since the district was listed as one of the most vulnerable districts in Sierra Leone after the devastating rebel war. Pujehun District Students’ Association (PuDSA) as a remarkable academic association has boosted unity among stakeholders and students through the attendance of our traditional events since the inception of the association, promotion of girl child education as a priority area of our intervention, advocating for scholarships and other financial assistance for the membership and vulnerable pupils form the district to enhance access to education; in the end, the effort and support to those needy students will be converted into career development programs and the reduction of poverty in the district and Sierra Leone as a whole.
